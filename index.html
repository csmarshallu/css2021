About CSS

The Computer Science Symposium (CSS) aims at bringing the professors, researchers, and students together in the fields of computer science, information systems, and cyber security to disseminate original research results, and share new ideas and emerging technologies through practical development experience concentrating on both theory and practices. CSS is a university-wide event that showcases students’ research and creative efforts.
