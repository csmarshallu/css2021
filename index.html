About CSSET

The Computer Science Symposium on Emerging Technologies (CSSET) aims at bringing the professors, researchers, and students together in the fields of computer science and information systems to disseminate original research results, and share new ideas and emerging technologies through practical development experience concentrating on both theory and practices.
